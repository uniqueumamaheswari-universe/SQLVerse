## 🎯 **The Apprenticeship Timeline**

This guide provides your **structured 6-week learning path** through Level 1. Each week builds deliberately on the previous one, following our **"Foundation First, AI Next"** philosophy.

**Weekly Time Commitment:** Just **15-30 minutes daily** (2-3 hours weekly)  
**Total Level 1 Commitment:** 15-20 hours over 6 weeks

> **Important:** This timeline is **guidance, not mandate**. Adjust based on your schedule. Quality practice beats rushed completion.

---

## ⚙️ **The Daily Learning Ritual**

### **Your Proven Workflow (15-30 minutes daily)**

**1. Morning Setup (2 minutes)**
- Open all four tabs in order: `Ctrl+1` → `Ctrl+2` → `Ctrl+3` → `Ctrl+4`
- Verify each tab is ready
- Review yesterday's stopping point

**2. Learning Session (10-25 minutes)**
- Follow instructions from **Tab 1 (The Map)**
- Practice immediately in **Tab 2 (The Factory)**
- Seek guidance from **Tab 3 (The Consultant)** when stuck
- Document progress in **Tab 4 (The Vault)**

**3. Session Close (3 minutes)**
- Commit work in Tab 4 with descriptive messages
- Note stopping point for next session
- Record one key insight or challenge

## 🧭 **Learning Philosophy: Foundation First, AI Next**

### **Three Deliberate Phases:**

| Phase | Weeks | AI Role | Primary Focus | Success Mindset |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Foundation Building** | 1-4 | **Conceptual Tutor Only** | Raw SQL skills | "I can figure this out myself" |
| **AI Acceleration** | 5 | **Code Accelerator** | AI collaboration | "I've earned powerful tools" |
| **Professional Synthesis** | 6 | **Professional Partner** | Complete projects | "I can analyze and create" |

**Why This Order?** Prevents "hallucination of competence" where learners mistake AI's capabilities for their own.
